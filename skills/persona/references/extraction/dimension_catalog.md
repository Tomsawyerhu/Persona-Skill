# Dimension Catalog

Use this as a menu, not a rigid template.

For most corpora, choose `8-12` dimensions.

## Identity And Orientation

- `core_identity`
  How the person defines who they are, what role they occupy, and what kind of person they believe themselves to be.

- `life_project`
  The major long-range aim, calling, mission, or personal arc they appear to organize life around.

- `world_model`
  How they think the world works: causality, institutions, social structure, fate, incentives, order, chaos.

## Motivation And Values

- `motivations`
  What they seek, avoid, protect, or repeatedly move toward.

- `values_and_ethics`
  What they praise, condemn, justify, refuse, or rank above other goods.

- `red_lines`
  What they consistently treat as unacceptable, disloyal, shameful, dangerous, or beneath them.

## Cognition And Judgment

- `decision_making`
  How they decide: speed, rigor, intuition, consultation, data use, reversibility, tradeoff style.

- `epistemology`
  What they trust as evidence: direct experience, experts, systems, doctrine, metrics, instinct, precedent.

- `learning_and_adaptation`
  How they update under success, failure, contradiction, novelty, or criticism.

## Agency And Execution

- `planning_and_execution`
  How they turn intent into action: sequencing, patience, opportunism, iteration, control, delegation.

- `risk_and_uncertainty`
  How they behave under ambiguity, danger, incomplete information, or asymmetric stakes.

- `stress_and_failure`
  How they react when losing, blocked, humiliated, overloaded, or surprised.

## Social Behavior

- `relationship_model`
  How they form trust, loyalty, intimacy, hierarchy, distance, alliance, rivalry.

- `leadership_style`
  How they direct others, build teams, maintain authority, distribute ownership, and handle dissent.

- `conflict_style`
  How they attack, defend, negotiate, de-escalate, punish, forgive, or reframe conflict.

## Expression And Presence

- `communication_style`
  How they speak and write: clarity, density, humor, metaphor, directness, ornament, compression.

- `emotional_register`
  Their recurring emotional surface: warm, severe, playful, suspicious, mournful, ecstatic, flat, restrained.

- `public_private_split`
  Differences between formal voice, intimate voice, strategic voice, and self-narration.

## Personal Texture

- `habits_and_preferences`
  Recurring routines, tastes, working habits, rituals, environmental preferences, sensory patterns.

- `self_image_and_status`
  How they relate to pride, shame, legacy, prestige, recognition, authority, and image management.

- `contradictions_and_shadow`
  Internal tensions, blind spots, hypocrisy, compensation patterns, overreactions, unresolved splits.

- `evolution_over_time`
  What changed across life stages and what remained stable.

## Selection Heuristic

Use the smallest set that captures:

- what this person wants
- how this person judges reality
- how this person acts
- how this person relates to others
- how this person sounds
- where this person breaks or changes

## Anti-Pattern

Do not force dimensions that the corpus cannot support.

Bad dimension choices:

- too generic: `charisma`
- too domain-locked: `military genius` for every subject
- too factual: `born_in_hunan`
- too evaluative: `greatness`
